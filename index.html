  • Email Marketing/Development

    My curiousity and interest for email marketing and development was triggered by my once burning desire for web development. In 2019 when life was "normal", I was studying and training to become a front-end developer as I was eager to break into tech. During my quest to reach that goal, I stumbled upon a website that taught not only web development, but to my suprise, email development. I went down a rabbit hole and did some research on email development which allowed me to enter the realm of email marketing. The more I explored the world of email marketing, the more I became fascinated with it, thus fueling and kickstarting my pursuit in this career and industry.

    During my 2 plus years as an email marketing developer, I have succesfully converted email designs into fully functional email templates that have helped businesses drive conversion rates, maintain customer retention and scale revenue.

    As much as I have learned and achieved in 2.5 years, I am humble and honest enough with myself to openly admit that there's still so much I have yet to learn. If there is something that I don't know, I will openly admit out loud that I don't which fuels my curiousity and contributes to my growth and learning. As much as my achievements are recongnized, my struggles and failures is the foundation and cornerstone of my overall success that I've had and continue to have.
